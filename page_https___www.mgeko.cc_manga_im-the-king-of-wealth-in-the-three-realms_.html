About Chapters

I’m the King of Wealth in the Three Realms is a Manga/Manhwa/Manhua in english language, Action series, english chapters have been translated and you can read them here. You are reading chapters on www.mgeko.cc fastest updating comic site. The Summary is

Lin Yuan accidentally inherits the divine position of King of Wealth across the Three Realms (Heaven, Earth, and the Underworld). In the mortal world, he starts by face-slapping his ex-girlfriend, dealing with village bullies, and becoming the wealthiest person—but that’s just the beginning. He takes his ambitions further by introducing revolutionary business concepts to the Heavenly Court itself: “Immortal Pill Mystery Boxes” and “Underworld Cultural Tourism,” using modern commercial business strategies to completely overturn the traditional economy of all Three Realms. When his disruptive innovations anger an ancient evil King and trigger a judgment from the Heavenly Court, Lin Yuan launches an earth-shattering financial and power revolution to prove one thing: the true King of Wealth doesn’t just know how to spend money—he can reshape the very laws of heaven and earth itself!
