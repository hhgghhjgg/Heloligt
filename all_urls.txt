                <div data-editor-viewer="1" class="_83r8m _2CKTq _3NjDa mDnFl"><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">The Fusion-Rain, this is what the event has come to be called that happened nearly 15 years ago. An event that would put the entire world on its head. It caused people to fuse with the DNA of their Pokémon. It’s been taught in schools as the way how the world came to be nowadays.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><strong>The History</strong></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Around 15 years ago, in a lab at an undisclosed location, an experiment went horrible wrong. It was always a bit of a mystery to scientists how exactly Pokémon got their power, and where they draw the energy from to call upon this power. They were able to control lightning, quake the earth, and even use shadows for offense. A few scientists came up with an idea, they would develop a way to fuse with Pokémon. If they fused with Pokémon, or at least had access to their powers. They could figure out where and how they got their power. Months went by, several experimentations with Ditto’s, research into evolution, and a few requests for funding to the board. Soon enough, the research was complete. A liquid was developed, it would find the nearest Pokémon DNA and copy it, then it would add that DNA to the nearest human target. It would give the human the powers Pokémon had, or at least, that was its intention.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Two pods were used, one had the Pokémon in it, and the other had a volunteer scientist, both under light anesthetics. In the middle, a tank with the liquid. Several tubes went between the two pods, that’s where the liquid would travel through. The experiment was ready to start. At first everything seemed to go as planned, the liquid bubbled up, and pressure was normal, but about halfway it went horrible wrong. As the liquid absorbed the Pokémon’s DNA, the pressure inside the tank rose without stopping. And it rose fast. As if the liquid itself wanted to escape. Hitting the emergency stop didn’t help, cracks formed in the glass and little drips seeped out through those cracks. Though, those drips were all that needed for the chain reaction. Some of the liquid dripped inside the electrical parts of the machine, it started to short-circuit and smoke. The anesthetic system shut down, and the anesthetics themselves wore off quickly. Both the Pokémon and Scientist woke up, both panicking, having no clue what was happening to the machine. Of course, a creature with such power did the first thing it could do to release itself; Unleash its power. This caused severe damage to the machine, the shockwave of the blast blew open the pod door, but also sent a large amount of pressure into the glass tank, already weakened thanks to the cracks. It shattered, spilling the liquid everywhere. At this point it was complete chaos. The machine had caught fire, the Pokémon that was helping with the experiment had panicked after waking up, the liquid had leaked, so many things at once. The scientist focused on shutting down the machine and calming the Pokémon down but failed to notice how the liquid seeped away through the damaged and cracked floors and walls of the experiment room. It seeped through the gaps and into the ground and into waste gullies. A lot of it got into the water waste pipes, ending up in an open reservoir. Here it mixed with the water.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">The region had a period of heavy draught while all of this was happening. Sun shining harsh in the sky, and the water reservoir vaporizing into clouds containing rainwater, and the fusion liquid. As the clouds got moved by the wind, the rain fell over the region. Landing in rivers, lakes, and the sea. And this is when the effects of the liquid were felt, and when the whole world turned upside down.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">People are often close with their Pokémon, playing with them, training them, or living with them. This of course sometimes leaves fur particles, saliva, or steel and rust particles in the case of steel Pokémon, on you. These particles can be obvious, like a white hair on a black shirt, or subtle, regardless they all carry DNA of said Pokémon. A good shower would wash it off. But how many had a shower in the mid-day after an entire morning and a good portion of the afternoon interacting with Pokémon. As the rain fell, the fusion liquid did only its intended purpose. Fuse Pokémon and humans. People started changing, they would turn mostly into the Pokémon they were closest with or had the most DNA of on them. That was one saving grace of this liquid, as it seemed to only pick the highest concentrate of DNA, which prevented horrifying amalgamations of fusions. The amount of DNA seemingly dictated how far someone would become a Pokémon. As you would expect, this created a mass-panic instantly. Everyone searched for shelter as soon as possible, but it wouldn’t help much. As soon as the liquid made contact with you, it would change you. The amount didn’t matter, even a single drop was enough. It only would take longer. More liquid means it can change you faster, and less means it’ll change you slower. Though, going out of the rain wasn’t enough, water supplies were affected too, and so tap water wasn’t safe to drink without changing.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">All the ruling bodies all over the world scrambled to contact each other, this event seemingly spreading quickly, and quite possibly affecting the entire world soon enough. They had to figure out what was going on, who was responsible, and how it happened. But first they had to focus on calming the civilians down. About half an hour since the incident happened, an emergency lockdown was issued, and the water supplies cut off.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">The lab quickly found out about the disaster that was happening throughout the region due an emergency alert that was sent to all phones. They were honest enough to quickly report themselves to the appropriate parties. They complied with every order and immediately shut the lab down. It took less than a day for the lab to explain what had happened to the government, and around 2 days to assess the damage.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">The damage to the building was relatively minor, the cracks in the walls and floor were an easy fix. Repairing the machine would take longer, but the biggest issue were the people. There was no intention of it being reversible, as it originally only would have affected the one volunteer scientist, the one who suggested this whole idea. As such, everyone affected was permanently stuck like a Pokémon hybrid. This would of course spark anger, confusion, and terror.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">But they had to tell the truth, right here and now. There was no time for sugar coated words. Any delay in answers could only increase the tension.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">On the fourth day after the rain, a worldwide broadcast was held, translated in all dialects and languages. They started off by apologizing to everyone affected, stating that everyone severely affected by their new forms that they can’t do their jobs, will be heavily compensated. And support centers will be constructed worldwide to get people to learn to use and control their new powers. Furthermore, the research lab would be shut down, and any and all research onward, worldwide, must be communicated with a special research committee, as well as other rules that was only important to the researchers. However, then came the part would most likely cause a panic, they had one of the scientists explain exactly what happened, why it had happened, and then… It’s permanency. People all over the world were obviously shocked, some happy, some scared, and they took it to social media to immediately voice their thoughts. But once the scientist was done, all the world leaders appeared on TV and announced research will be done in turning people back to normal if they wanted to. But for now, new laws were in place. Government offices would be open for longer and more staff would be there to help with any document changes, job hunting or welfare. Human doctors would also immediately be required to learn Pokémon Healthcare, as such, Pokémon Centers and human hospitals started becoming one and the same. But also new rules were stated for trainers, and the Pokémon League. All Pokéballs had an update sent to them. This update prevented the PokéHybrids, as they were called at this point, to be caught. Thanks to the Pokémon DNA mixed with them, they were now capturable. Though new Pokéballs were also distributed that were specifically designed to be able to capture PokéHybrids, but they would ONLY work if the person the ball was thrown at wanted to be able to be caught. Otherwise, it would just bounce off. And with these new laws in place, the world as it is now, was created, with some small changes over the years. After about 4 years everyone mostly forgot about even changing back, the research was still on, but demand was low, and so it got pushed more and more to the back, until it was mostly forgotten about. And society became as we know it today.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><strong>The Change</strong></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">While it wasn’t the intention. The Poké-Rain was a perfect test and demonstration on how different amounts of DNA would affect people. As the Fusion Liquid would fall onto people, it searched for any Pokémon DNA on their body. If there were multiple DNA samples present, it would pick the highest concentrate of DNA. After that, it would mix the DNA with the Human’s DNA. The amount of changes, and exactly what changes, depends on the amount of Pokémon DNA. Some stayed more human than Pokémon, others became a good 50-50 mix of both Human and Pokémon, and others changed to be more Pokémon than human. What features remained more human was more of a gamble, there was no logic or pattern, it was random. Fully dependent on the amount of DNA, and how the Liquid decides to rewrite the DNA. Though there were trends that seemed to happen to mostly everyone regardless of the amount of DNA. It is speculated that these are the necessary components to use the power of Pokémon, as everyone was also able to use moves.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">First of is the head; Everyone would get the head of the head Pokémon, with low and medium amounts of DNA it is possible that their previous hairstyle might even be present a little bit. However, there are also cases of people keeping a human-shaped head, but with very heavy Pokémon characteristics. Like horns or ears.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Second is the ability to talk to Pokémon; What is suspected to be a change associated with the head, the ability to talk with Pokémon is something guaranteed anyone gets if they change. Although there is still debate on how this is possible to this day, as the Fusion Liquid was never intended to affect the brain. Some say it has to do with an alteration to the vocal cords, some say a mutation in the Liquid did make it so it affects the brain, and others claim it’s because Pokémon were affected by the Liquid too. Although that last claim is often waved away as there is no proof that the Liquid has an effect on Pokémon. However, it is noted that the ones with a medium-high to high concentration of Pokémon DNA, have more trouble speaking human language. Though accounts vary on why, some say they’ve been using Pokémon Speech so much they’ve forgotten some human words, some find using their Pokémon speech much more comfortable, and others find pronouncing human words has gotten harder, quite possibly due their new mouth structure.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Third one is a human body shape; Since it was meant to turn the affected person into a hybrid, a very large amount of people still have their human body shape. This includes breasts, weight, and butt. The arms, hands, legs, and feet were more random. There are also reports of people being more Pokémon shaped, for example people who were fused with snake Pokémon, they had the lower body of a snake. Or the ones fused with spider Pokémon. Though there was a chance for more muscle mass for people with muscular Pokémon. And depending on the fusion, people could also have tails or wings. But what everyone always had, was that their body had the color, and sometimes texture in the case of rough skin, of the associated Pokémon. Height was also a factor sometimes affected, people could shrink or grow taller. Though not by much usually.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Fourth were the hands and feet; While not as common, human shaped hands and feet were still a thing to most people luckily. And with them, human legs and arms. Their nails were usually in a color that was on their body. Though having human legs didn’t always mean human feet, especially for the ones with dog Pokémon, their feet were often more like paws. Their hands also could be more human shaped, but still possess paw pads and claws. The same goes for those with bird Pokémon, scaly feet and sharp fingernails. And sometimes there were less toes on the human feet.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Fifth, and finally; Reproductions organs. These stayed human-like very often, aside from being a different color. But there was one major difference, they were compatible with both Pokémon AND humans, and everyone laid eggs instead of the usual procedure, though there was still an obvious belly when one was developing an egg. The kind of creature that came out, also highly depended on who or what impregnated the carrier. A pure Pokémon would result in a pure Pokémon egg, a PokéHybrid would result in a PokéHybrid child of the same species as the mother, and a human would result in a human. Though those would be born as normal, without the need of an egg.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">There was one more change that was probably just as shocking, if not even more so, than becoming a Pokémon hybrid, Gender. There were Pokémon that were female-only or male-only. As such, males affected by a female-only one, would change into a female. And vice versa. Then there were Pokémon that were in a ratio of more females than males or more males than females, in this case there was a chance to become the majority gender, although often one stayed the same gender. But there were also instances of people ending up as having both working male and female characteristics, like breasts, a vagina, and a penis. People with a fusion of a 50-50 gender ratio seemed to remain unchanged in gender. And then there was the genderless Pokémon. While for these people their reproduction organs seemed mostly intact, there was a very, very, very small chance of becoming infertile, and it’s incredibly rare for it to happen.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Then there was the matter of evolution. While it is unclear how exactly evolution is triggered, it is possible for people to evolve. Most commonly this seems to happen with age and mental maturity. Though it also can happen through battle. They don’t lose any human features upon evolving.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Of course, such big changes called for an update in documentation. Government administrations ran overtime. A new system had to be created to be able to register everyone’s fused species, their human features, and new fingerprints. Medical personnel, who were previously only trained in human anatomy, were now trained in Pokémon anatomy as well. Blurring the line between Pokémon Centers and human hospitals. Any people whose job was affected was helped by finding a new fitting job as well.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Community and support centers were built where affected people could talk about how the change has affected their life. They can learn how to control their powers, or even learn new moves. And those who had switched gender could also receive support here.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Cars and public transport were adjusted for the different types of people. All in all, for how unexpected and quick this change came. Society managed to adapt rapidly. And soon enough really, the need to reverse the changes was pushed more and more to the back while more and more people forgot about it and just accepted this new body. After all, it wasn’t all bad. They could finally talk with their Mons, they could use powers which would be useful in day-to-day life, they often felt more energetic, and they couldn’t get sick anymore as easily. To some, it was a blessing in disguise.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">We talked earlier about how the liquid only picks out the highest amount Pokémon DNA it can find on an affected person. But there is one exception. Should a person have the exact same amount of DNA of two or more Pokémon right in up in the decimals. Say a 50-50 precise split. It is possible for the person to get characteristics of all those Pokémon and may result in a fused version of the Pokémon. However, these cases are extremely rare as it has to be one the 5 decimals percentage exact.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><strong>The Laws</strong></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">With people now having new powers, new laws had to be put in place as well to keep the order. These laws would be upheld worldwide and affected both the league and society in general.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">The first obvious law was; Powers are not to be misused for crimes or harm. Doing so would be seen as a high priority crime. Since the powers allowed for more power, misuse of these powers had to be treated as very high danger.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">A law that affected the way battles went, was that the PokéHybrid can be a combatant themselves, but this would mean they can only carry 5 other Pokémon with them, as they counted as one Pokémon.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Speaking of carrying Pokémon, it was also highly illegal to “catch” PokéHybrids. All Pokéballs received a remote update to block catching PokéHybrids and special balls were creature that could capture them. But they would only work if the PokéHybrid themselves wanted to be captured, otherwise they would just bounce right off. Anyone that caught one against their will, was judged the same as human trafficking.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Any medical attention in a Poké Center that could only happen to a Pokémon, would be free to PokéHybrids as well if they needed it.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">There were also new laws in place when it comes to flying or levitating to ensure the air space wouldn’t get too clouded.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">While Hybrids were partially Pokémon. They still had to wear clothing and public nudity was not allowed due still visible reproduction organs. As such, clothing stores were also required to provide suitable clothing for such individuals.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Then there was the matter of marriage and Pokémon breeding. Marriage between a human and a PokéHybrid was still legal, and still regarded as a marriage between humans. But, since the Hybrids with also compatible with normal Pokémon, the ancient question arose; Should marriage and breeding between Hybrids and Pokémon be legalized? In ancient times, it was normal for humans and Pokémon to marry. As they were one and the same. But over time people saw the differences and while it was never actually outlawed, it became more taboo. Hybrids bridged the gap between the two. Ultimately, it was decided it became legal as Hybrids and normal Pokémon could fully understand each other, and so there was a good chance for understanding, and proper love. But ONLY between those two. However, there would be a lot of paperwork, and the Pokémon had to fully consent and ensure that they knew what they were signing up for, any unconsented breeding or marriage was punished with up to 25 years of jail. Though even after 15 years, none seemed to have regretted its decision.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Finally, a law that was introduced after some wanted to connect more with their Pokémon side. They didn’t want to live as a human anymore, shed it off completely. There was a lot of debate, and it’s probably, together with the marriage, one of the heaviest debated laws… Or rather, option. The option to strip yourself from your human rights and giving you the option to live as a Pokémon. To get this, one must go through several psychological tests, medical examinations, and paperwork. They must read up and even take an exam to ensure they know what it means. Then they must have a session in court to once again make their point. If the court and medical results rule them as sound. Then the process begins, they’d have to give up their belongings, house, cars, money, everything. They can choose what happens to it, donated, sold, going to family, and so on. The same goes for money, though the money can also be sent to the local government if needed. All their accounts will be frozen and deleted. The only things that remain are their medical records and governmental records. Locked away in a secure vault and secure servers. After that, they can say goodbye to their family if they want, or if they’re going to live with someone, go with that person. There’s one sure-fire way to not get permission however, and that is if there’s a criminal record. Since they don’t want to risk a criminal not being able to get prosecuted like a human. Though of course this doesn’t apply if the crime was something small like stealing some candy.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">There are of course more laws, but these laws are the standing pillars of the modern-day community.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><strong>Modern Day</strong></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">And now we are in the modern day. And society is thriving. Humans, Hybrids, and Pokémon now living together in harmony. Society goes on the same as before the incident. Schools have extra curriculums to learn about Pokémon. Competitions now feature one on new Hybrids battles. There are popular Hybrids bands and actors. New way for healthcare with Hybrids being able to use moves like Aromatherapy for example. Post delivery is faster thanks to the Hybrids with wings. Some Hybrids characteristics helped in the beauty world. Even the adult entertainment had profited with certain Hybrids. Thanks to some research, there was even a special kind of serum called the “Ditto Drink” which makes the Hybrid drinker able to temporarily change their body in any way. Gender, Weight, Height, or Size, that sort of thing, by being able to use some of Ditto&#x27;s power that was ingested via the drink. Normal humans could use it too, though it was much harder for them than a Hybrid who could use Pokémon moves. And while a way to revert was still being researched, it had been pushed to the back more and more, and nowadays people don’t even care about changing back. And the world’s governments are working closer together than ever before.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">But the Fusion-Liquid is still in the waters, though not any of the tap water. Luckily, those already affected can’t be affected again. But anyone new that comes in contact with the liquid, MUST report to a special medical center immediately. There they must stay for a little while until the liquid is extracted from them. Either via body sweat, or bodily waste. This to, eventually, eradicate every trace of the liquid in the world’s waters. Water supplies are now equipped with more advanced filters. But it’s still recommended to avoid swimming in lakes or puddles of rain due this liquid, unless you don’t mind changing.</p></div>            </div>

It&#x27;s a place born out of the amalgamation of human thoughts, intellect, concepts, and ideas. Here, thousands of ideas have come to life, thousands of realities and worlds connect, here... gods live. Outside of the outer edge of the universe, was nothing. When the first cavemen told their stories by fire, painting on the walls of their sanctuary; a small rift was formed, a disturbance and a portal to our universe. As more cavemen followed, more started painting their walls, they started creating concepts like spears, as humanity evolved; The rift started getting bigger. Growing into it&#x27;s own mini-universe.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">When the first choice was made, one born from free will instead of instinct, the rift became unstable. It warped, closed and opened, until it eventually split into three parts. One was the original rift, the one before the choice was made, the other two were the results of the two choices, a reality where one choice was made, and one were the other one was made. Connected by an unknown cosmic energy, something even greater than the four forces of the universe. As more major choices were made in each reality, more rifts were made, growing out like a web. In the center being the first rift. Then, gods were made, starting with the first human who thought of their existence, their concept. They started out as a small sphere, the human&#x27;s thoughts and ideas stored within, their vision. More and more people became aware of their concept, their existance. And so the first of the Alzo&#x27;og were born.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">The Alzo&#x27;og is the name used for the godlike beings that roam this mindspace. Their true form is incomprehensible to humans, for as the Alzo&#x27;og describe it; “To have a form outside of reality, you must not exist but also exist, to see and be blinded, to be yet to be born, to be alive, and to be dead at the same time”. And their language would probably melt the eardrums of any human. However, this does not mean the Alzo&#x27;og have no forms, or can not speak. The small spheres of concept and ideas are called “The newborns”. Ones that get enough recognition as a concept or as a deity, are given form, named “The shapen ones” in our tongue. But they&#x27;re not conscious. When a shaped one is given even more recognition, enough to get a consciousness of their own and change their own form, they&#x27;re called “The awakened”. These are Alzo&#x27;og that float through the mindspace freely on their own, they&#x27;re able to look on at the different realities, but unable to interact. There are only a few strong enough to directly interact with the all the possible realities and rifts. These are called “The prominents”, they are well known by the humans, whether as a god or as a concept. These are the most powerful of the Alzo&#x27;og, even able to create their own pocket rifts. In their own pocket rift, they can adjust it to their own liking, these are often called “Realm Rifts”. Though their powers are usually dependent on their concept. But most notable of all, they&#x27;re able to enter all rifts. Though their presence can cause a disturbance in that rift. For the Alzo&#x27;og do not abide by most Rifts’ universes. Usually they go on planets, either as gods or as humans, making sure their concept or view as a deity is kept alive. For being known, is their source of power. There is a stage between being an Awakened and becoming a Prominent, they are called “The arising”, for they&#x27;re making their way to power. While they can&#x27;t enter rifts, they can interact with its inhabitants. Mostly as a voice in their heads, or as a vision in a dream, with the goal of spreading the knowledge of their existance. Though some humans go mad, after all, who can comprehend a being not bound by reality. Then finally, there are “The Fallen”. These are the Alzo&#x27;og that have been forgotten. As one Alzo&#x27;og can rise to power, so too can one fall from power. For human concepts and creativity is what keeps them alive. Should they be forgetten, whether as a deity or as a concept entirely, they will revert all the way being a small sphere of ideas again, until completely forgotten by all, in all rifts, in all realities. Then they will cease to exist. Although with the amount of rifts this is rare, it&#x27;s not impossible to happen.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Our universe isn&#x27;t the only rift in existance of the Mindspace. When the first human starting creating a world in their mind, another seperate rift opened up. One not connected to our rift. The only connections it had, were small balls with characters inside of it. Similar to “The newborns” and yet different, for these do not expand into Alzo&#x27;og&#x27;s but rather stay connected to their own world and its laws, as they are part of it. Indeed, The mindspace is not only home to a rift to our world, but also home to all the worlds humanity has come up with over the years. Just like in our rift, The Prominents are able to enter and affect these worlds, as if they were different universes. As such, even if humanity forgets their concept in our rifts. If they exist in these worlds, they will almost always live forever onwards. As such, the Alzo&#x27;og have a name for our rifts, and the worlds of fiction. “The Ectrifs” and “The Vakrifs”, the Ectrifs being our rifts, and the Vakrifs being the rifts of fictional worlds. Unlike our rifts, fictional worlds rarely split with decision making for their decisions have been written for them. There are only two known ways for Vakrifs to split. Either an Alzo&#x27;og interacts and influences the world enough for a reality to appear with their influence, and one with none of their influence. Or we have to change those worlds. Known by us as fanfiction. Take a world that is plunged with war for example, if we rewrite that world as a fiction where war never happened, it&#x27;ll split and have two rifts. One with and without war. The connected spheres with characters are called “Karsphirs”, much like the Vakrifs, can be interacted with and can cause a split in the Vakrifs. Their backstory can be altered, their personality, and even their looks. However, changing them will not change the story connected to the world. If an Alzo’og changes a character too much to the point of not being their original self anymore; The new Vakrif will collapse in on itself, the altered characters connection to the rift severed. For how can the story continue in its proper order, if the character is non-existant? This new character will soon dissappear after their connection is severed. For this new character does not exist in the human mind.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">You might be asking yourself; “If these rifts are created by the human mind, our creativity, our ideas, our concepts. What happens to concepts that are similar?” They get absorbed into one. Take the concept of direction, left and right. Left is different from right, and right is different from left. Two different directional concepts, and yet they&#x27;re concepts of direction all the same. As such, they get absorbed into one. Only if they&#x27;re different enough, will they be separate. Like two different sides of a coin, still make one single coin. Different and also not. And then you might be asking; “If we create rifts based on our concepts and ideas, can the Alzo&#x27;og create rifts as well by thinking up concepts?”. And the answer is that they can, yet I will not be able to tell you how, for much like their appearance or their sound, their concepts are beyond us.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Now, I trust you have enough knowledge of the mindspace and the beings that are within. And if you notice anything out of the ordinary, an Alzo&#x27;og might have been at play.</p></div>            </div>

                <div data-editor-viewer="1" class="_83r8m _2CKTq _3NjDa mDnFl"><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Keralyn stretches out her arms above her head before lowering them and swinging them side to side a bit. In front of her was a muddy track of at least several kilometers long. She was participating in something known as “the mud race”. Hundreds of competitors entered a race in a muddy track, simple, you might say. But there is a catch. When the first person crosses the finish line, everyone else still in the race will start to turn into a pig, the more you&#x27;re behind, the faster you change. If you managed to cross the finish line then the changes would revert, except for some gained weight. The ones who became fully transformed before they could reach the finish, would live out their rest of their days as a pig. If they didn&#x27;t have anyone to take care of them, then they would be sent to one of the farms that were sponsoring the event.</p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px"><br/><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">Keralyn looked around as she stood at the starting line wearing her dark blue sporting shorts and grey tank top along with her black running shoes, her hair tied in a ponytail. There were many different kinds of people participating; young, old, people from all kinds of backgrounds and all genders. She took up the starting position as everyone else got ready, the announcer counting down “3... 2... 1...” and then... BANG. With a loud noise the start signal was given and everyone ran off. Everyone wanted to finish first and so ran at full sprint. Keralyn stood behind a bit to conserve energy and she would sprint when everyone else got tired even if this meant risking to be turned into a pig faster.</p><p class="empty-p"><br/></p><p style="text-align:left;margin-inline-start:0px">The first few kilometers went well, despite the trouble of getting through the thick mud, people were seemingly getting tired from the sprint at the start and she managed to overtake a few of her competitors. That&#x27;s when her body started to get warm “Crap, did someone already finish!?” Keralyn thought to herself, she didn&#x27;t expect someone to finish already this early on. She picked up the pace but she was still far behind.
